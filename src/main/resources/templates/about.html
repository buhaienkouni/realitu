Who we are?

  Welcome to Realit.U, a platform dedicated to important stories and news related to the war in Ukraine. We tell and show real stories of everyday heroes, people whose fortitude and determination inspire us every day.

  We not only share relevant news, but also pay special attention to the stories of people whose lives have been touched by the war. Our Realit.U project provides an opportunity to financially support those in need by raising funds for their real needs.

  We are proud that Realit.U is independent of sponsors. However, in order to keep the platform running and growing, we take a small percentage of the funds raised to support the system. This allows us to remain independent and continue to help those in difficult situations.

  Join our community to learn and support the real stories and people who, against all odds and war, continue to fight and give hope. Realit.U is a place where every story matters.
